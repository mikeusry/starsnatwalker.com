# Recruiting Webinar — April 28, 2026 (TheAllianceFP × Sports Recruits)

**Panelists:** Adam Glickman (Firecrackers), Tori Lewis (Lady Dukes / Blue Magic), Donny (Finesse Fastpitch)
**Source:** https://x.com/thealliancefp/status/2047334946922983773

## TL;DR (3 sentences)

Spring is the work; summer is the harvest — coaches show up to events with a "shopping list" already built, so being on it requires emails, video, school research, and camps done **before** June. Once a coach is at your game, you've already passed the athleticism bar; what they're evaluating is character, body language after failure, leadership, and how you handle a strikeout — not your slash line. NIL and rev-share for softball are mostly noise (98% of programs have crumbs), the transfer portal has tightened roster spots, and the strongest filter against the chaos is honest evaluation: cast a wide net (D1 through NAIA / JUCO), pick schools by fit not name, and "drive your own bus" — though clubs disagree on how hands-on they should be.

## What We're NOT Doing (deliberately)

- **Not adopting the hands-off "just drive the bus" framing.** That's how clubs avoid accountability. We take the heat.
- **Not hyping NIL.** Don't want parents picking schools chasing rumored money.
- **Not promising D1.** Wide net or nothing.
- **Not running mass emails to coaches.** Every email is targeted, with a tracked link, and a specific reason for sending.
